WITNESS STATEMENT - Case #1987-4521
Date: March 16, 1987
Witness: David Rodriguez, Age 28
Location: Apartment 3B, 847 Oak Street

I was watching TV in my apartment when I heard a loud argument outside around 9:25 PM. I looked out my window and saw two men arguing in the parking lot. One was wearing a dark jacket and jeans, the other had on a light-colored shirt.

The argument got physical. The man in the dark jacket pushed the other man, and then I heard what sounded like a gunshot. The man in the light shirt fell to the ground. The other man ran to a car - I think it was red or maybe burgundy - and drove off quickly.

I called the police right away. I couldn't see the faces clearly from my window, but the man in the dark jacket was taller, maybe 6 feet or more. The whole thing happened so fast, maybe 2-3 minutes total.
