POLICE INCIDENT LOG - Case #1987-4521
Date: March 15-16, 1987

21:42 - Dispatch received 911 call from Margaret Chen reporting man down in parking lot behind warehouse at Oak Street and 5th Avenue.

21:47 - Officers Martinez and Johnson arrived on scene. Victim unresponsive, gunshot wound visible. Paramedics called.

21:52 - Paramedics arrived. Victim pronounced dead at scene.

21:55 - Crime scene secured. Detective Sarah Morrison notified.

22:15 - Detective Morrison arrived. Began witness interviews.

22:30 - Witness Margaret Chen interviewed. Reported seeing red sedan fleeing scene, possible license plate starting with "JK" or "JX".

22:45 - Witness David Rodriguez interviewed from apartment 3B. Reported hearing argument and gunshot, saw suspect flee in red/burgundy vehicle.

23:10 - Crime scene processing began. Shell casing recovered near dumpster.

23:45 - Victim identified as Robert Mitchell, 34, local resident. Next of kin notified.

March 16, 1987
00:30 - Crime scene processing completed. Evidence logged and transported to lab.

08:00 - Detective Morrison issued BOLO (Be On Lookout) for red sedan with dented rear bumper, license plate possibly starting with "JK" or "JX".

14:00 - Forensics report received. .38 caliber bullet recovered. Red paint chips found on victim's clothing.

CASE STATUS: ACTIVE INVESTIGATION
Lead Detective: Sarah Morrison
Priority: HIGH
