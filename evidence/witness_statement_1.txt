WITNESS STATEMENT - Case #1987-4521
Date: March 15, 1987
Witness: Margaret Chen, Age 42
Location: Corner of Oak Street and 5th Avenue

I was walking my dog around 9:30 PM when I heard shouting coming from the parking lot behind the old warehouse. I saw a red sedan speeding away, heading north on Oak Street. The car had a dented rear bumper on the driver's side. I couldn't see the license plate clearly, but I think it started with the letters "JK" or "JX". 

There was a man lying on the ground near the dumpster. I immediately called 911 from the payphone on the corner. The man wasn't moving. I stayed until the police arrived around 9:45 PM.

I remember the car because it was driving erratically, swerving between lanes. The engine was very loud, like it had a modified exhaust. I've lived in this neighborhood for 15 years and never seen anything like this before.
